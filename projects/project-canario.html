Overview

From idea to delivery

Project Canario analyzes operational challenges at a web agency known for high-profile websites. The report highlights pain points such as manual testing, risky releases, limited automation, and weak version control that threaten reliability and competitiveness.

The proposed solution modernizes the technical infrastructure with Git/GitLab, CI/CD pipelines, feature flags, and cloud hosting. A gradual transition plan is outlined to improve efficiency and operational excellence without disrupting ongoing work.
